Which is the best JEE Main and Advance Preparation Book?
There is no specific book that is considered to be best for JEE Main Preparation, but if we talk about the most preferred BOOKS then, HC Verma and I E Irodov is for physics, M L Khanna, and Arihant for Mathematics, and JD LEE and Solomon & Frhyle for Chemistry.
Which BOOKS are JEE Main toppers recommended using?
Best Recommended BOOKS By Toppers for JEE Main Preparation are, HC Verma and I E Irodov is for physics, M L Khanna and Arihant for Mathematics and JD LEE and Solomon & Frhyle for Chemistry.
How much NCERT is sufficient for JEE Preparation?
NCERT will help you in Chemistry major parts and somewhere in Physics and Mathematics too. But you cannot depend only on NCERT.
Which is the best JEE Main Math BOOKS?
Objective Mathematics by R D Sharma, Plane Trigonometry by S L Loney, TMH, and Algebra by Dr. S K Goyal Arihant Publications.
Which are the best JEE Main Physics BOOKS?
H C Verma - Concept of Physics, Fundamentals of Physics - Halliday & Resnick, Daniel Kleppner & Robert Kolenkow - Introduction to Mechanics, and Vibrations and Waves - A.P French.
Which is the best JEE Main Chemistry BOOKS?
Paula Yurkanis Bruice - Organic Chemistry, P Bahadur - Physical Chemistry, and J D Lee - Concise Inorganic Chemistry.

How many hours should I study every day for JEE?
There is no fixed number of hours that you should study every day. It depends on your grasping power and your daily routine. However, it is recommended to study for at least 6-8 hours every day.
What are the most important topics for JEE?
The most important topics for JEE are Mechanics, Electrodynamics, and Modern Physics in Physics; Physical Chemistry, Organic Chemistry, and Inorganic Chemistry in Chemistry; and Algebra, Calculus, and Coordinate Geometry in Mathematics.
How can I improve my problem-solving skills?
You can improve your problem-solving skills by practicing as many problems as you can. You should also try to solve the problems without looking at the solutions.
How can I manage my time during the exam?
You can manage your time during the exam by attempting the easy questions first and then moving on to the difficult ones. You should also keep a track of the time and try to complete the exam within the given time.
What should I do on the day of the exam?
On the day of the exam, you should stay calm and focused. You should also have a good breakfast and reach the exam center on time.
How can I deal with exam stress?
You can deal with exam stress by practicing yoga and meditation. You should also take regular breaks and get enough sleep.
